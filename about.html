Dr Sabine Selchow

I am Senior Research Fellow in the ERC-funded project 20th Century International Economic Thinking and the Complex History of Globalisation at the European University Institute, Florence. Before that I was a researcher at LSE Global Governance and in the Civil Society and Human Security Research Unit at the London School of Economics (LSE), including as Research Fellow in Mary Kaldor's ERC-funded project "Security in Transition" and in Ulrich Beck's "Methodological Cosmopolitanism: In the Laboratory of Climate Change" (based at LMU, Germany). I have extensive teaching experience, including at the LSE and Sciences Po, Paris.

I have a PhD in Government from the London School of Economics (LSE).
