Participants of the World Economic Conference 1927, Geneva

This dashboard presents a structured dataset of delegates and committee/sub-committee memberships at the World Economic Conference (Geneva, 4–23 May 1927). The data is compiled and curated as part of the European Research Council (ERC) grant ECOINT, Grant No. 885285.
Sources: League of Nations, Report and Proceedings of the World Economic Conference held at Geneva, May 4th to 23rd, 1927, vol. II (Geneva, 1927), C.356.M.129.1927.II.
