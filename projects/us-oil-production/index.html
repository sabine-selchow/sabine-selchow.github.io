Oil: U.S. Total Production, Imports and Annual New Supply (1918-1943)

Date: 27 September 2025

Source: United States, Office of Naval Petroleum and Oil Shale Reserves (1945). Twentieth century petroleum statistics. Washington, p. 22

Funded: Part of the European Research Council (ERC)-grant ECOINT, Grant No. 885285

This visualization shows the trajectory of U.S. oil production and imports between 1918 to 1943. It documents domestic production and two different kinds of imports.
